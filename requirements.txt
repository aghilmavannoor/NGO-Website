6. Design Requirements
•
•
•
•

One consistent logo and colour palette throughout
Simple navigation (5–7 main menu items maximum)
Real photographs only — no generic stock images in the final version
Accessible design: readable fonts, sufficient colour contrast, alt text for images

7. Handover Requirements
• Domain, hosting, and CMS login credentials handed over to the NGO
• Basic training / walkthrough video for content updates
• Written agreement on post-launch support duration and cost of future changes
